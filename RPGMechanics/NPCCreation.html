NPC Creation

NPCs (Non Player Characters) are a bit different from player characters. They require less information, but they can be a hassle to create. (An NPC generator is on its way, don't worry.)

The following lists the basic requirements for any NPC.

Stats

Stats are generated the same way as you would generate a players stats.

Skills

Skills are up to the GM, however it is reccommended you give the NPC a class and refer to classes for skill generation.

Equipment

This is optional, but in most cases NPCs should have a weapon and some money.
