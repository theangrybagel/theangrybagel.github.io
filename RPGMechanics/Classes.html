Classes

Pilot

Pilots are excelent at flying and piloting spaceships and other vehicles. Most space people have the basic skills of a pilot, but to be considered a pilot is to be a master, with many G-Years of training, at operating vehicles.

Skill Boosts
Heavy Spacecraft Operation - 2 | Spacecraft Operation - 3 | Planetary Vehicle Operation - 2.5 | Galactic Knowledge - 1.75 | Spaceship Engineering - 2 | Situational Awareness - 1.5


Mercenary

Mercenaries are good at combat. It's pretty simple. They like to fight. That's it.

Skill Boosts
Small Weapons - 2 | Large Weapons - 2 | Ranged Weapons OR Melee - 3 | Dodging - 2 | Weapon Engineering - 1.75


Hunter

Hunters generally make a living either hunting animals, or hunting other space people for large amounts of money.

Skill Boosts - Tracking - 3 | Ranged Weapons - 2.5 | Small Weapons or Large Weapons - 2.5 | Situational Awareness - 2.5 | Stealth - 2
