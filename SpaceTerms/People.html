People

Here is a large list of space people and things they've done.


Hudson Yorkston - Invented modern space travel. They've got several books on it. Yorks, a unit of long distance measurement, was named after this person.

Zahf Pauletto - Founder of the most popular religon in the galaxy.

Vladimir Koh - Founder of the Galactic Federation

Vatoya Rudkin - A spy for the Galactic Federation back in 35603 AGF.

George Blois - A DOWRI scientist, responsible for the invention of Serkium-1218K.

Delisa Durand - An engineer, biologist, and a colleague of Hudson Yorkston. This person is best known for their creation of the Jump Map Navigation Network. This person was also the first person to document Eldon Foot Fungus, as well as the first to die from it.

Deacon Gantry I - The initial leader of the the Divine Oligarchy.

Deacon Gantry JR Alpha II-???? - Several leaders of the Divine Oligarchy have existed, but none of them have achieved anything significant enought to make it onto this list of people.

Sheena Stilman - Founder of Carton Industries.
