Ship Terms

Unit

A unit of fuel.

Fuel Type (FT)

A type of fuel. For more info on fuel types, click here

Fuel Capacity (FC)

Amount of fuel that can be stored.

MYPLLU

Microyorks per low level unit

YPHLU

Yorks per high level unit

MPM

Microyorks per minute

YPM

Yorks per minute

Intended Cargo Capacity (ICC)

Intended capacity for cargo (total capacity of things like cabinets and closets on the ship)

Jump Admission Cost (JAC)

The cost to use a Jump Wheel.

Weapons Class (WC)

The class of weapons that can be mounted to a ship. Click here for more info.
