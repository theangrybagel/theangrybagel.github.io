Space Books


Space Books are a little different from your typical book that you'd find at a local library. Space books are books that have become famous throughout the galaxy, being published in several different ways by several different publishers. Due to many publishers being extremely lazy, the books that they publish often end up being several times shorter than the original book.

The Concept Of Space Travel

Written by Hudson Yorkston

A book about space and how space works and more things about space.

ROTU

Written by Zahf Pauletto

A religous text about some dude named ROTU, which is an acronym for Ruler Of The Universe. This religion has instilled many cults throughout the galaxy.

History Of The Galaxy, Volume I

Several Authors

A common textbook used in many space academies.

History Of The Galaxy, Volume II

Several Authors

A more up to date history book, with events from 50,000 AGF to 80,000 AGF.

Timelines

A list of timelines for different information.

Large Collection Of Journals

Several Authors

A collection of random journals found and documented throughout the galaxy.
