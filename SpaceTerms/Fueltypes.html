Fuel Types

Identifying Fuel Types

A fuel type will typically start with FT-, followed by a symbol representing that fuel type, then followed with either -LL or -HL (representing low level or high level fuel types.)

Low level fuels are typically used for short distance travel. For instance, when traveling from one side of a planet to another, low level fuel should be used.

High level fuels are used for travel between planets in a single solar system. An average ship can generally travel accross a solar system within half an (earth) hour.
