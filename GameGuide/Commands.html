Commands/Cheats

The developer console can be opened with [Enter]. The console can be used to spawn items, AI, objects, and change the time, and access other useful information such as positional data.

Spawning items

Items can be spawned using /give [itemtype] [itemname] [amount]. Options for types include weapon, armor, and ammo. You can also spawn other things like tools with /givefrompreset [itempresetname]. This will load itemdata from StreamingAssets/Data/Presets/Items. and give it a new GUID.

Other Commands

/pos - Returns your positional data.
/time - Returns the game time.
/ignore - Causes all AI to ignore your presence.
/invincible - Gives you infinite health.
/setpos [x] [y] - Sets your position.
