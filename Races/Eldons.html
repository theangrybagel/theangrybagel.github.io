Eldons

Known for their age, this race is by far the oldest race in the galaxy, possibly even the universe. Eldons are capable of living millions of years, and remembering just about everything that happens in their life. Due to their long lives, they advanced quicker than any race and may also be the most intelligent lifeform in the galaxy. In addition to the results of these long lives, Eldons reproduce once every four GY. The excessive amounts of offspring forced them to migrate to other planets, which is why they control majority of the Eldron Sector (Sector 29). The Eldons first started their civilization on Ubirhiri Mu XVI, dating back all the way to 50,000BGF, and they rapidly began to expand outside of Thuyowei Gamma XIX around 12000 AGF.

Lifespan An Eldon can live for 300,000 to 1,190,000 GY
Health Eldons have absolutely no health problems except for their weakness in their middle toe on their right foot. Almost every Eldon has this problem, and it causes them to complain far more than what most cultures would consider reasonable. On some planets, it is illegal for an Eldon to complain about their feet, which has caused a great deal of EFF on these planets
Prefered climate Eldons tend to live on very, very cold planets with little to no plant life. The simple thought of plants has been known to cause nausea in Eldons that lasts for 17GY.
Climate Limitations
  • Eldons hate the sun. Many of their planets have a Sun Sheild to keep the sun off their planets. This helps prevent plants from growing as well
  • Eldons require a very thin atmosphere to breath. When on any space outposts, if you see an Eldon, it's likely that they will have an atmospheric radiator to help them breath.
