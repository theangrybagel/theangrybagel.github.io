Torgians

Torgians are very annoying to interact with. They are typically known for their stubborness and need to control everything. Chances are, if you see a Torgian at your local space mall, there's more of them, and they intend to harm someone. 87.6% of the Torgian population works for the Torgian Military, however only 43% of them actually wear their military uniforms, which is why any Torgian could be potentially dangerous, because all they want is universal domination, which includes dominating your local space mall.

Lifespan Torgians generally live up to around 110 GY.
Health Torgians don't have many noteable health problems although they have been known to transmit SRMS.
Prefered climate Torgians only live in methane and carbon atmospheres, and there are no other known climates that can support them. There are many theories in relation to what planets a Torgian can live on, and nobody has ever been able to test these theories because every Torgian is stubborn, and cares far too much about themselves to risk their own health on behalf of these theories.
Climate Limitations
  • The only climatical limitation known to the galaxy that relates to Torgians is their stubbornness when it comes to moving to a different planet.
