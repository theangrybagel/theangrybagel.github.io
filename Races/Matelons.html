Matelons

The Matelons are one of the many common species in the galaxy, and they joined the Rebel Peasants in their fight against the DO in 602.7 AGF. They're known for their eyes that exist underneath their face. Yes, they have skin that goes over their eyes.

Lifespan Matelons usually live for about 193GY.
Health Matelons have been known to be very insensitive to light, for they started on a planet that was only 2.5 Yorks away from its sun.
Prefered climate Matelons prefer rather dry and hot enviroments.
Average Height/Weight 7 FT / 250-320 LBs
