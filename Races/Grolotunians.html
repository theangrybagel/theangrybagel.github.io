Grolotunians

Grolotunians are known for their skills in flying spacecraft, which is why many space people will often choose a Grolotunian as their co-pilot. They have two hearts, one of which is very close to the brain, which makes them great at flying spaceships upside down while still in a planets atmosphere.

Lifespan Grolotunians live for 60-120GY.
Average Height/Weight 5-6 FT / 150-200 LBs
