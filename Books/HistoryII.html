History of the Galaxy, Volume II


71349 AGF

In the year 71349, the Divine Oligarchy launched a rather unexpected attack to the planet of PXF-9, located in the Epsilon CBF-2 star system. It started in 71349.241, when the DO launched a raid on the Rebel city Frareington, which was a neutral trading ground for many people on PXF-9. The event was a massacare, and millions died on this day.
