The Concept of Space Travel

Written by Hudson Yorkston


Space

Space is fundamental to the universe. We can move, because of space. We can see, because of space. We can do things, because of space. In this book, I will, as you can probably already tell, be explaining space.

Communicating space

Space can be difficult to explain if there aren't any points of reference. I have invented several forms of measurement - Yorks. A fun fact for you bored readers out there - I determined the size of a York by calculating how far my body would stretch if it were ripped apart, atom by atom, in a very large black hole, and each atom was placed in a long line. Anyways, back onto Yorks. They're a measurement of distance, best used to describe the distance between to objects in space. Then, there are microyorks. These represent one millionth of a York.
